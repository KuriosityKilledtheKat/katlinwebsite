About

Hi! I'm Kat Lin

Armed with a biomedical engineering degree from Yale, a Yale Journalism Scholar certification, and a lifelong love of writing, I turned my attention into filling that frustrating gap between medical professionals and laypeople.

That's how I've become so experienced at writing in a way that translates difficult medical concepts into simpler terms. I know my way around medical peer-reviewed papers and terminology. After all, I've (1) collaborated with a team and a physician to produce a project that was accepted as a poster presentation to the BMES/FDA National Conference in 2017, (2) made a bioreactor for 3d printed blood vessels for a Yale medical school lab, and (3) finished the entire biomedical engineering degree.
