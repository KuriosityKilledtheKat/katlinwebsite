       Kat Lin hates defining herself or being defined.
But since this is a bio page, for our purposes, we'll say
that she is a nonfiction writer and photographer...

       She defected from the world of biomedical
engineering after graduating with a Bachelor's and
currently lives semi-nomadically as she galavants around
the world while sometimes being paid to do so.

        She aspires to be a more well-rounded experimental storyteller and hopefully one day, will somehow manage combine her writing, photography, digital media editing, engineering background, and choreographic experience.
