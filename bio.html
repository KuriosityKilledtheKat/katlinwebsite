Kat Lin hates defining herself or being defined. But for our purposes, she is a nonfiction writer and photographer... and aspriing experimental storyteller who hopes to one day somehow combine her writing, photography, digital media editing, engineering background, and choreographic experience.

She defected from the world of biomedical engineering after graduating with a Bachelor's and currently lives semi-nomadically as she galavants around the world while sometimes being paid to do so.
